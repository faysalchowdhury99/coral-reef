Introduction

Coral Reef is a social network and enterprise that will be built on the blockchain in the form of a Decentralized Application (DApp) where women from all walks of life can converge to develop relationships beyond the borders of their country. The internet has been for a few decades and smart phones have been become popular during this past decade. With the recent global pandemic, we believe it is time to use the power of our ability to connect, to strengthen, and highlight what makes every woman similar and celebrate our differences in a borderless manner without even leaving our respective countries. Welcome to the Reef!

Why Coral?

There are several websites that cater to women in general, from forums, to story sharing of self growth, to childbirth experiences, women go online to find support groups for many situations. We look for places and people who have had similar experiences and share them freely even without knowing each other personally, we strive to help one another because we know we’ve been there before. With all the available networks and network speed in the world, we harness the next generation of the web to create a place for women to find their global tribe. To the best of our knowledge, there is no known app of this nature until now. A new tribe will be born – The Coral Tribe.

Our Features

What we can offer

The Coral DApp will have a marketplace that allows users to open up their own shop and sell to other women. This gives them a chance to build their own wealth while paying a small fee to the platform.

The G-Chat section will be a community of forums. But most importantly, it will allow the ladies to chat anonymously using avatars to discuss freely about topics that matter. Contributors in the ecosystem will also be rewarded with tokens called Seashells (SSL)

From period tracking, to ovulation tracking, to steps per day and managing weight loss goals, the Wellness Tracker feature is designed for women to be able to go to one single app and track information that is important to their lives and how they chose to live it.

Given the current realities of today and global medical tourism being valued at $44.8 Billion in 2019, the G-Expert Corner provides women with a convenient option. The G-Expert Corner is designed to be a plug in for health professionals and counselors to schedule appointments for women looking for virtual consultations for take ins and get medical advise for everything from body image to fertility discussions and options.

10% Social Good

10% of the precedes generated from the entire platform will be dedicated to social projects and supporting women in developing countries to become financially independent. Such initiatives includes:

Provide women in developing countries with basic technology education
Providing girls of all ages with sanitary items
Providing school gadgets and supporting school fees
Grants to support women from developing countries to brand and market their own products on the coral platform
